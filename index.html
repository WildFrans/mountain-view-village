The Estate

Home To

Mountain View Village Estate is a thoughtfully designed gated residential development in Robertson — one of South Africa's most celebrated valley towns, known for its wine estates, warm community, and spectacular mountain scenery.

Located on the corner of Paul Kruger Street and Waterkant Street, the estate offers freestanding homes on 270m² stands that combine quality construction, smart design and the peaceful rhythm of Boland small-town living.

Whether you're looking for a permanent home, retirement destination, or a sound investment in the Western Cape interior, Mountain View Village is where your next chapter begins.
